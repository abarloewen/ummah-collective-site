The brand

Köln’s mobile
matcha bar.

Matcha Madness brings premium organic matcha, whisked live at a beautifully styled mobile bar, right in front of the guests. Founded by Nour Khammar, it turns weddings, henna evenings, corporate events, fairs and summer parties into a calm, ceremonial moment — across Köln, Bonn, Düsseldorf, Leverkusen and Bergisch-Gladbach. Our brief: give a fast-growing catering brand a home as premium as the experience itself.

The build

One brand,
seven languages.

We rebuilt matchakoeln.de from the ground up: a cinematic, mobile-first site that speaks seven languages — German, English, Arabic (right-to-left), Turkish, Bosnian, Albanian and French — auto-detecting each visitor’s own. At its heart is a booking wizard that turns a browser into an enquiry in four steps, with a live price estimate, plus email integration so every request lands directly in the inbox. Local SEO, a seven-post blog and a full brand kit round it out — now live on their own domain.

The booking engine

Four steps to an enquiry

The wizard is the site’s engine. Instead of a dead contact form, guests are guided — package, flavours, event details, contact — and see an instant price estimate before they send. The finished enquiry is emailed in full, so Matcha Madness can reply with a quote in minutes.

  • Schritt 1Paket wählen — S, M oder L
  • Schritt 2Sorten & Extras auswählen
  • Schritt 3Event-Details: Datum, Ort, Gäste
  • Schritt 4Kontakt + Sofort-Preis, per E-Mail
