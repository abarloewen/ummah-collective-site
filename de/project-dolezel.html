The brand

Four decades
of Munich Handwerk.

Adolf Dolezel GmbH has secured Munich since 1980 — a family-run Meisterbetrieb on Weissenburger Straße in Haidhausen, trusted by over 10,000 customers for keys, locking systems, safes and alarm technology, with a ★4.9 Google rating to show for it. The craft was never the problem. The web presence was — a dated site that sold a Munich institution short. Our brief: a digital storefront as solid as the workshop behind it.

The build

Bold, modern —
and built to sell.

We rebuilt dolezel.de as an eight-page Bold Modern system — Inter-led typography, ink and petrol, hard grids — around the three Säulen of the business. At its centre: an online shop with cart and click & collect, a nine-article Ratgeber that answers real security questions, and the shop’s Google reputation pulled into the site itself — verified reviews, ★4.9, real workshop photography. Shipped and live on Vercel.

What we built

Redesign · shop · content

Bold Modern design system

Inter-led typography, ink and petrol, hard grids — a 40-year Handwerk brand that finally looks as solid as it builds.

8-page website

Start, Unternehmen, Leistungen, Produkte, Shop, Referenzen, Blog, Kontakt — one coherent system, fully navigable.

Online shop

12 products with category filters, cart and click & collect — ordered online, picked up in Haidhausen.

Ratgeber blog

Nine practical articles — from burglary protection to choosing a safe — with filters and pagination.

Reputation, front and centre

Real Google reviews with verified badges and the ★4.9 rating built into the experience, not hidden on a third-party site.

Social discount mechanic

A 5% follower discount tied to Instagram and TikTok — turning store visits into an audience.

The digital storefront

Bestellen · abholen · sparen

A local locksmith doesn’t need a webshop for its own sake — it needs one that brings people into the store. The Dolezel shop is built around click & collect: order online, pick up at Weissenburger Straße, get advice face to face. The 5% follower discount closes the loop, turning walk-in customers into an owned audience.
