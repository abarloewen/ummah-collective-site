The specialist

Protection is personal.
So is trust.

Hj. Asyraf Fauzi is an authorised Zurich Takaful specialist and Agency Director of Gemilang Empire ZFN — serving Kuala Lumpur and the East Coast for 10+ years, twice Champion Personal Producer (2022 & 2023), with an RM1M agency contract volume in 2021. Takaful is bought on trust and explained face to face — his web presence needed to open that door, in the customer’s own language.

The build

BM first.
Numbers up front.

We built the site Bahasa Melayu–first — with English and full right-to-left Arabic behind a switcher, 242 keys per language. Two things do the converting: a four-step booking wizard that turns interest into an appointment, and four live calculators — medical, hibah, education, motor — that give a family real numbers before the first conversation. Staged on Vercel, ready for go-live.

What we built

Site · wizard · calculators

Trilingual, BM-first

Bahasa Melayu by default — the customer’s language — with English and full right-to-left Arabic, 242 translation keys each.

Booking wizard

A four-step flow — service, meeting type, date & time, details — ending in a clean email-style confirmation.

Four takaful calculators

Medical cover, family protection (hibah), education savings and motor — live estimates before the first call.

Achievements as trust

Champion Personal Producer ’22 & ’23 and the RM1M agency milestone — built into a dedicated trust page.

One-pager + multipage

Two architectures from one system: a fast single page for ads, five structured pages for depth.

WhatsApp-first contact

Every CTA routes to WhatsApp or the wizard — no dead forms, no friction.

The booking engine

Four steps to an appointment

Insurance sites die at the contact form. This one guides instead: pick a service, choose how to meet, pick a slot, confirm — and the enquiry arrives structured, ready to answer. For everyone else, WhatsApp is one tap away on every page.

  • Langkah 1Pilih perkhidmatan — takaful plan or advice
  • Langkah 2Jenis pertemuan — WhatsApp, call or in person
  • Langkah 3Tarikh & masa — pick a slot
  • Langkah 4Butiran & pengesahan — email-style summary
