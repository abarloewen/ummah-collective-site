The brand

Trust is
the product.

Almaruf Maid Agency places carefully selected, fully documented helpers with Malaysian families — 3,000+ families over 10+ years, licensed by JTKSM, JIM and KBRI, and led by director Puan Zunainah Mohammed. In this industry, trust is everything — and their old website carried none of it. Our brief: a digital home that earns a family’s confidence before the first phone call.

The build

One agency,
four languages, two brands.

We rebuilt the site as seven fast, mobile-first pages speaking English, Bahasa Melayu, Bahasa Indonesia and Arabic — full RTL included, 232 translation keys per language. Every call-to-action opens a pre-filled WhatsApp chat, because that is where Malaysian families make decisions. And alongside the maid agency we built Almaruf Cleaning — a green-identity service line with Sertu & Samak Islamic purification as its differentiator.

What we built

Site · languages · software

Website rebuild

Seven pages — home, services, cleaning, about, testimonies, FAQ, contact — rebuilt clean, fast and mobile-first.

4 languages, full RTL

English, Bahasa Melayu, Bahasa Indonesia and Arabic — 232 translation keys each, with proper right-to-left Arabic.

WhatsApp-first conversion

Every CTA opens a pre-filled WhatsApp chat — the channel Malaysian families actually use. No dead contact forms.

Almaruf Cleaning sub-brand

A second service line in its own green identity — six cleaning services plus Sertu & Samak Islamic purification.

CRM مخصّص

Purpose-built operations software: helper registry, placements pipeline, permit renewals and cleaning jobs.

Trust architecture

Licences (JTKSM, JIM, KBRI), guarantees, real testimonies and the director’s own story — structured to answer every doubt.

The operations engine

Custom CRM, built for this business

A maid agency lives and dies by documents and deadlines — passports, permits, FOMEMA checks, embassy contracts. Off-the-shelf CRMs don’t speak that language, so we built one that does: every helper, placement and renewal in one system, with automatic alerts 60 days before anything expires.

  • Module 1Maids & Helpers registry — passports, permits & FOMEMA expiries
  • Module 2Placements kanban — seven stages, enquiry to handover
  • Module 3Renewals & permits — automatic 60-day due alerts
  • Module 4Cleaning jobs board — the second business line, same system
