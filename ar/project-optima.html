The platform

Invoices in.
Cash flow out.

Optima Limited gives SMEs, exporters and suppliers what banks make slow: working capital. Businesses submit unpaid B2B invoices and receive an advance through a fully digital platform — transparent, contract-based, with no hidden fees, and with Optima handling the customer follow-up. Ethical financing, engineered as a product.

The build

For a fintech, the website
is the first credit check.

Optima came to us with a product and nothing around it. We built everything from scratch: the brand and CI, the UX of the application journey, a dark fintech UI with violet depth and product mockups, the five-page website, and the SEO and content that explain invoice financing in plain language. When a company asks SMEs to trust it with their cash flow, the site has to answer first — this one does.

What we built

Brand · UX · UI · web · SEO

Brand & CI from zero

Name treatment, mark, colour world and voice — a fintech identity built from a blank page.

UX architecture

From first visit to submitted application — the apply flow designed before a single screen was drawn.

Dark fintech UI

A near-black system with violet depth and product mockups — credibility you can see before you read a word.

Website build

Five pages plus the application flow — product, company, apply and contact, all built from scratch.

SEO & content

Plain-language explanations of invoice financing — structured for search and for humans skimming an FAQ.

Trust architecture

No hidden fees, real-trade backing, an FAQ that answers the hard questions — trust engineered into copy and layout.

The product, made legible

Upload · approve · get paid

Invoice financing sounds complicated — which is exactly why the site refuses to be. The whole product fits in three words on the page: Upload. Approve. Get Paid. Everything else — FAQ, principles, process — exists to remove the next doubt before it forms.

  • Step 1Upload — submit your unpaid B2B invoices
  • Step 2Verify — invoices checked on the platform
  • Step 3Funded — capital disbursed within days
  • Step 4Follow-up — Optima manages the customer, not you
